Project Overview & Details

This app was a quick project, done with a limited brief and a within very short time frame. I completed it start to finish in about three hours between other projects.

For this app, I kept my focus on something minimalist but functional, designed to be comfortable and intuitive to use an functional but feature light.

Project Details

Tools
  • Figma

Timeframe: ~3 hours, over a couple days

Design Proccess

Due to the simple nature of this app, I chose to start my proccess right in figma, working through a greyscale wireframe for the layout after looking at some other examples of notes apps. Note taking apps are fairly simplistic in terms of what they need to be functional - you really just need somewhere to write and view your notes.

I chose to a trash bin with recently deleted items to help prevent mishaps, and the ability to pin and archive notes to help with organization.

After I'd mocked up a simple, clean layout with all the needed functions and planned pages, I moved right into colours and fonts.

Colours & Fonts

I chose a warm, earthy palette for this app, aiming to imitate the feeling of a cozy leather notebook. I chose red as a warning colour, to make things like deleting pop for ease of use.

  • Dark Chocolate #361F0A
  • Chocolate #6B533C
  • Caramel #BD915D
  • Grey #D3CFC5
  • Beige #E7E2D8
  • White #FFFFFF
  • Red #B7281F

Typography

  • Headers and Titles (Inknut Antiqua)

  • Body (Anadada Pro)

I continued with this theme for fonts, choosing something with personality for headers, and using a very simple, easy to read serif font for the body.

Components

While working on this app, I wanted to make sure I was defining my colours and components for ease of editing, and for clarity. Each component has multiple states, making modiyfing colours, and protoyping, easy.

Final Screens

Conclusion

This project was a great exercise in working rapidly to take a concept to the simplest viable finished product.

Overall, the project went well, and I'm quite happy with it - I'd like to protoype this design in the future, which will be simple enough to do due to the steps I took with my components.
