About Zoe

My Story

Where are you based? I live in Guelph, Ontario.

How do you spend your free time? When I’m not drawing (I’m usually drawing), I love being outdoors. I’ve been horseback riding since I was a kid, so I love being around the ranch, as well as going for walks in the woods and just enjoying nature in general. I’m also a big fan of video games, especially rhythm games and open world exploration.

What’s a fun fact about you? I essentially live in a little zoo! I love animals, and have had pets all my life - I never feel right without them. Right now I have a ball python, leopard gecko, three hermit crabs, a pacman frog, a toad, a vinegaroon, and two tropical fishtanks.

What's your prior experience? I have little professional experience in UI/UX or web design just yet, but it’s something I’m excited to get properly in to. I started designing websites as places to share and organize my art and characters, and found it to be very enjoyable work - so I looked into education surrounding it and began building my skillset and portfolio. I love finding creative and functional ways to share information and tell stories, and web design is a natural extension of that for me.
