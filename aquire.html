Aquire is a targeted, highly specific storefront designed for aquarium hobbyists. The app’s design is bright and friendly, using bright colours and a white background to make the product photos (in this case, mostly of aquarium livestock) pop. Users will have the ability to sort stock by type, price, colour, water temperature range, and minimum tank size, allowing for users to find what they’re looking for with ease.

Additionally, Aquire will provide direct delivery to homes, as well as to affiliated pet store locations for pick up as an alternative option. Users can also go to these affiliated locations for any returns.

Aquire’s basic style guidelines:

  • Friendly:
    the app needs to feel personable and playful
  • Inviting:
    the store should feel welcoming and lively
  • Bubbly:
    this ties to the earlier themes, but also, the rounded shape of bubbles, as well as many of the fish being sold (livebearers and goldfish), to create harmony
  • Clean:
    the app should feel clean and minimal despite use of bright colours and photos

Ideation & Approach

I chose to approach Aquire from a visual-design first direction, placing strong emphasis on the colours. A storefront should be clean, simple, and easy to use - but that doesn’t mean it shouldn’t stand out. I spent a lot of time with the colours to ensure that they do some heavy lifting when it comes to making the user experience pleasant.

Orange as a key colour not only draws the eye well, but will always contrast nicely with the green and blue backgrounds of most livestock photos.

Wireframes

Creating wireframes for this app was relatively simple, as the final app was always intended to use high contrast blocks of colour and white to create an appealing, clean design.

High Fidelity

I was happy with my first set of high fidelity wireframes; however, after some feedback and critique, I felt that the header was a bit cluttered and unbalanced.

I feel the narrower header with the counterbalanced menu options/search bar feels much more level, and less intrusive, while still adding colour and character to each page.
